--- title: Busted BCD rendering slug: Busted_BCD_rendering ---

There was a time when the old Compat macro didn't inject the "BCD query" as an id attribute on the <div class="bc-data"> tag.
This would cause problems in Yari when it tried to break up the page into sections.

The whole point of this sample fixture data is to verify the fix for https://github.com/mdn/yari/issues/2785.
